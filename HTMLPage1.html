    Beef Burger

    Step 1 - In a large bowl, combine the beef, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, ground mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
    Step 2 - Mix all ingrediant and using your hands form the meat into 4 large meatballs.
    Step 3 - Heat pan to medium-high heat and coat pan with oil.
    Step 4 - Place burgers on skillet and firmly smash them flat with spatula.
    Step 5 - Cook for about 2mins, salt the burger, flip the burger, add cheese and cook for another two mins.
    Step 6 - Prepare Bun and Enjoy!

    Turkey Burger

    Step 1 - Combine ground turkey, breadcrumbs, dried onion flakes, ketchup, Worcestershire, garlic, and salt into mixing bowl.
    Step 2 - Using your hands mix the ingrediants and form 4 equal size turkey balls.
    Step 3 - Heat pan to medium-high heat and coat pan with oil.
    Step 4 - Place the turkey balls on skillet and smash with spatula.
    Step 5 - Cook turkey burgers for approximately 5 mins on each side.
    Step 6 - Prepare Bun and Enjoy!

    Vegan Burger

    Step 1 - Stir TVP into boiling water in a medium mixing bowl, add keetchup, Worcestershire, soy, and salt.
    Step 2 -Let stand for 10 minutes.
    Step 3 - Slowly add flour until the mixture holds its shape.
    Step 4 - Using your hands make 4 balls out of the mixture.
    Step 5 - Heat pan on medium-high heat and coat pan in oil.
    Step 6 - Cook until both sides are hardened up and darkened brown.
    Step 7 - Prepare bun and Enjoy!

    Creamy Potato Salad

    Step 1 - Submerge potatoes in water, bring water to boil and simmer while covered for 30 minutes.
    Step 2 - Drain pot, let potatoes stand until you can safely cut potatoes into bitesize cubes.
    Step 3 - Mix mayo, vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper in large mixing bowl.
    Step 4 - Add potatoes to mixture with celery, onions, eggs and sprinkle in paprika.
    Step 5 - Refridgerate until ready to serve.
    (Atleast 2 hours)
    Step 6 - Enjoy!

    Seasoned French Fries

    Step 1 - Slice potatoes into French Fries.
    Step 2 - Heat oil in saucepan to 350 degrees F.
    Step 3 - While saucepan is heating, add flour, garlic, salt, onion salt, and paprika to a large mixing bowl.
    Step 4 - Slowly add water to the mixture until you get a thick batter.
    Step 5 - Dip fries in the batter and then carefully move them to the hot saucepan for about 8 minutues.
    (Don't add to many fries to the pan or they will clump up and not cook evenly).
    Step 6 - Remove fries and place on large cookie sheet lined by paper towels.
    Step 7 - Serve and Enjoy!

    Vegan Potato Salad

    Step 1 - Submerge potatoes in water, bring water to boil and simmer while covered for 30 minutes.
    Step 2 - Drain pot, let potatoes stand until you can safely cut potatoes into bitesize cubes.
    Step 3 - Add vegan mayo, sweet pickle relish, mustard, celery and paprika to a large mixing bowl and stir until creamy.
    Step 4 - Add boiled potatoes to the mixture and stir.
    (Add salt and pepper as needed)
    Place salad in refridgerator until ready to serve
    (Atleast 2 hours).
    Step 5 - Serve and Enjoy!
